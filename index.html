ABOUT

Named after the sixth President of the Philippines. Quirino is a land-locked province in the Cagayan Valley Region in Luzon. The province borders Aurora to the Southeast, Nueva Vizcaya to the West and Isabela to the North. In the past, Quirino (pronounced as “KIRINO”) used to be part of Nueva Vizcaya until it was separated in 1966. Quirino is one of the country’s youngest in terms of foundation. The youngest Province in Cagayan Valley Region II consist of only six municipalities namely Cabarroguis (its capital), Diffun, Saguday, Aglipay, Maddela and Nagtipunan.
